How the Game Works

Layers Roll combines simple mechanics with strategic depth to create an engaging casual gaming experience. Your journey begins with a basic wheel that you'll continuously build throughout each level.

The core gameplay loop revolves around color matching. As you roll forward automatically, you swipe left or right to position your wheel over colored strips. Successfully matching colors adds layers to your wheel, making it larger and more valuable. However, you can only collect strips that match your current wheel color.

Color gates strategically placed throughout levels temporarily change your wheel's color, opening up new collection opportunities. Timing your movements through these gates is crucial for maximizing your layer count. Meanwhile, obstacles like stripping surfaces and solid walls threaten to reduce or completely stop your progress.

The game rewards planning and quick reflexes equally. You must look ahead to anticipate color changes while reacting swiftly to avoid hazards. At each fork in the path, weigh your options: take the safer route with fewer coins, or risk losing layers for greater rewards?

Upon completing a level, you enter the bonus zone where your wheel gradually loses layers as you continue rolling. The distance you travel directly correlates to bonus coins earned. These coins unlock new characters, adding variety and personalization to your gameplay. The bigger your wheel entering the bonus zone, the more rewards you'll ultimately collect.
